INTERNATIONAL ARRIVAL

Dubai International Airport (DXB) is one of the busiest airports in the world, serving as a major hub for international travel. It operates flights to over 240 destinations across six continents, facilitated by more than 100 airlines.


The airport features three terminals, with Terminal 3 being dedicated exclusively to Emirates and Qantas flights. DXB is renowned for its efficiency, extensive duty-free shopping, and state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring a seamless travel experience for millions of passengers annually. Its strategic location and comprehensive connectivity make it a pivotal gateway between the East and West.
